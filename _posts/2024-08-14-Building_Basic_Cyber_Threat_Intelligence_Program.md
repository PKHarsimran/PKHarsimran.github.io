### Types of Cyber Threat Intelligence

CTI comes in different flavors, each serving a specific purpose:

1. **Strategic Intelligence:** Offers a big-picture view of potential threats. It helps you understand broader trends and the overall threat landscape. This is especially useful for making decisions at the executive level. üß† (You know, the big-picture thinkers.)
